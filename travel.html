Discover Mandalay

Mandalay, the cultural heart of Myanmar, is a city where history and spirituality seamlessly intertwine. Founded in 1857 by King Mindon as the last royal capital, it stands as a testament to the country's rich monarchical heritage and deep Buddhist roots. Today, it serves as an essential destination for travelers seeking to explore majestic palaces, sacred pilgrimage sites, and the authentic pulse of Burmese life.
